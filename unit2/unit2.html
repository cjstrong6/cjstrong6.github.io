Best Dnd Races With Best Classes

Page 1 Page 2 Page 3
  • Wood Elf
    1. Druid
    2. Ranger
    3. Fighter

    Wood Elves are just an all around great race. They get a dex buff and they have ability to go into a trance when resting only requiring half the time to gain the effects of a long rest. If you design a well rounded Wood Elf with one of the listed classes, you will have a agile and deadly character.

  • Owlin
    1. Wizard
    2. Fighter
    3. Monk

    Owlins are amazing. First off, they look like humanoid owls. I mean, thats pretty awesome all by itself. They also get a plus 2/1 or a plus 1/1/1 to the ability score of your choice. Oh, did i mention that they have a flying speed equal to your walking speed?
